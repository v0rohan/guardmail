FAQ

Fair questions

Can GuardMail read, send, or delete my email?

It can only read. We request Gmail's read-only scope, which makes sending, deleting, or modifying anything technically impossible. Google enforces that, not just us.

Do you store my messages?

No. Messages are fetched and scored on demand while you're using the dashboard, then discarded. The only thing kept is a record of emails you explicitly report.

What if it flags something legitimate?

Every score comes with a "Why this score?" breakdown, and one click marks a sender as safe so it's never flagged again. You always have the final say.

What does it cost?

Nothing while we're in beta. No credit card, no trial countdown, and the demo doesn't even need an account.
